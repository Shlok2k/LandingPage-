Frequently Asked Questions

We hope this FAQ helps answer your questions. If you need more information, please feel free to contact us.

What is the duration of the training and internship program?

The duration of our training and internship program is 1, 2 & 3 month.

What topics will be covered during the training?

The training program covers key concepts and practical applications in the chosen domain. This includes theoretical knowledge, hands-on projects, and live training sessions conducted by industry professionals.

Will I receive a certificate upon completion of the internship?

Yes, upon successful completion of the training and internship program, you will receive 3 certificates recognizing your internship, training and evaluation report.

What kind of projects will I work on during the internship?

During the internship, you will work on real-world projects related to your domain of study. These projects are designed to give you practical experience and help you apply the skills learned during the training..
